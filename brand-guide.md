# Brand Guide — Matt's Potats

**Version 1.0** | Food Truck Website Project

---

## Brand Identity

**Name:** Matt's Potats  
**Tagline:** *Spud-tacular eats on wheels.*  
**Alternative tagline:** *Potatoes. Elevated.*  
**Brand personality:** Warm, bold, playful, confident, approachable. The friend who makes really good food and knows it.

---

## Logo

The logo consists of two elements used together:

1. **The Badge** — A circular medallion with a cartoon potato character (two eyes, a smile, steam wisps rising from the top). The outer ring is dark brown (`#5C3D1E`), inner fill is amber gold (`#F4A624`), and the potato illustration is cream (`#FFF8F0`).

2. **The Wordmark** — "MATT'S" in a smaller weight above, "POTATS" in large bold display text below. Set in a heavy sans-serif (Arial Black or equivalent).

**Logo file:** `logo.svg`

### Logo Usage Rules
- Always maintain the proportions of the logo — don't stretch it
- Minimum size: 120px wide (don't use it smaller than this)
- Preferred placement: top-left of navigation bar
- On dark backgrounds: use the full-color version
- On light backgrounds: the full-color version works well; avoid inverting the potato badge

## Color Palette

Use these exact hex values for all design work.

### Primary Colors

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| **Amber Gold** | `#F4A624` | Primary brand color. Logo, accents, highlights, CTA buttons. |
| **Potato Brown** | `#5C3D1E` | Secondary color. Nav bar, headings, dark sections, footer. |

### Accent & Support Colors

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| **Spicy Orange** | `#E05B20` | Call-to-action accents, badges, "hot" label indicators. |
| **Warm Cream** | `#FFF8F0` | Page background, light section backgrounds. |
| **Peach Tint** | `#FFE8C8` | Alternating section backgrounds, card backgrounds. |
| **Deep Roast** | `#1A0E05` | Footer background, maximum contrast dark. |
| **Body Text** | `#2C1A0E` | All paragraph text. Warm dark brown, not cold black. |
| **Muted Text** | `#7A6050` | Secondary text, captions, descriptive copy under headings. |

### Color Don'ts
- Don't use a cold, gray-toned black (`#000000`) for text — use `#2C1A0E`
- Don't use bright primary red or blue — it clashes with the warm palette
- Don't use white (`#FFFFFF`) as a background — use Warm Cream (`#FFF8F0`) instead

## Typography

### Display / Headings
**Fredoka One** (Google Fonts)  
- URL: `https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Fredoka+One`  
- Use for: Page titles, section headings, the logo wordmark, CTA buttons  
- Style: Friendly, rounded, bold. It's fun without being childish.  
- Fallback: `Arial Black, Impact, sans-serif`

### Body / UI Text
**Nunito** (Google Fonts)  
- URL: `https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Nunito`  
- Use for: All body copy, nav links, menu item descriptions, labels  
- Weights used: 400 (regular), 600 (semibold), 700 (bold), 800 (extrabold)  
- Fallback: `Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif`

### Import snippet (paste into your HTML `<head>`):
```html
<link href="https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Fredoka+One&family=Nunito:wght@400;600;700;800&display=swap" rel="stylesheet">
```

### Type Scale (suggested)
| Element | Font | Size | Weight |
|---------|------|------|--------|
| Hero headline | Fredoka One | 64–80px | 400 |
| Section title | Fredoka One | 36–48px | 400 |
| Card heading | Fredoka One | 20–24px | 400 |
| Nav links | Nunito | 15px | 700 |
| Body text | Nunito | 16px | 400–600 |
| Labels / eyebrows | Nunito | 12–13px | 800 |
| Prices | Fredoka One | 20–24px | 400 |

## Brand Voice

### Tone
Confident. Casual. A little cheeky. Matt loves potatoes and isn't afraid to let that show.

### Words We Use
- "Build your potat" (not "customize your order")
- "Spud-tacular" (use sparingly — it's a good one)
- "Load it up" (not "add toppings")
- "On the truck" (not "in the restaurant" — we're a truck!)
- "The [Name]" for signature builds (e.g., "The Baked Boss", "The Buffalo Bomb")

### Words We Avoid
- "Artisan" / "Craft" / "Elevated cuisine" — too stuffy
- "Customizable options available" — too corporate
- Excessive exclamation marks!!! — one is enough per section

### Sample Copy Tone
> ✅ *"It started with a dare. Matt's friends bet him he couldn't make potatoes exciting. Three years and ten thousand satisfied customers later — he's still collecting on that bet."*

> ❌ *"Matt's Potats is a premier food truck establishment offering customizable potato-based cuisine options for discerning palates."*

## Iconography & Imagery

### Emojis as Icons
For the website, food emojis work great as lightweight icons:
- 🥔 Potato — use for the brand, logo fallback, navigation
- 🍟 Fries — bases category
- 🍗 Chicken — proteins category
- 🧀 Cheese — cheese category
- 🥫 Sauces category
- 📍 Location
- 📅 Events / Schedule

### Photography Direction (when photos are available)
- **Overhead shots** of loaded builds work best — shows the toppings clearly
- **Warm, natural lighting** — no harsh flash photography
- **Messy is good** — the food should look like you just made it, not a sterile product photo
- **Show the truck** — the truck itself is part of the brand story

### Placeholder Images
Until real photography is available, use:
- Solid color blocks in `#FFE8C8` with the potato emoji centered
- Or royalty-free food photography from Unsplash (search: "loaded fries", "food truck", "tater tots")

## Spacing & Layout

- **Section padding:** 80px top and bottom on desktop, 48px on mobile
- **Content max-width:** 1100px, centered
- **Border radius:** Use generous rounding — 16px to 24px for cards, 50px for pill buttons
- **Cards:** White background, subtle box-shadow (`0 4px 24px rgba(92,61,30,0.08)`), 20px border-radius
