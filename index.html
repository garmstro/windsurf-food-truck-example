How It All Started

Our Story

It started with a dare. Matt's friends bet him he couldn't make potatoes exciting. Three years and ten thousand satisfied customers later — he's still collecting on that bet.

What began as a joke became an obsession. Matt spent months perfecting his fry game — testing bases, nailing the crisp, dialing in the seasoning. Then came the toppings. Proteins. Sauces. The combinations were endless, and that was the whole point. Why settle for one way when you could build it your way?

The truck hit the streets of Kansas City in 2021, and the rest is spud history. From Power & Light District regulars to catering gigs across the metro, Matt's Potats has become the place people go when they want something that actually tastes like it was made with care. Because it was.

We're not fancy. We're not trying to be. We're just really, really good at potatoes.
